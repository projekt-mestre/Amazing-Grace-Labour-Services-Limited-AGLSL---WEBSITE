About us

A Few Words About Us

Amazing Grace Labour Services Limited (AGLSL) is in the business of labour sourcing and recruitment, targeting the sole purpose of placing labour in suitable organizations not only in Uganda, but also regionally and internationally. Incorporated on the 25th of July 2016, the company is officially registered and licensed by the ministry of Gender, Labour and Social Development to recruit and export labour.

The company’s staff members are well qualified with expertise indifferent disciplines and are always ready to give excellent customer care and professional services to the job applicants’ clients and in handling corporate relationships with government, Non-Governmental Organisations (NGOs) and other institutions, both locally and internationally. The company at all times ensures that the government of Uganda labour laws are strictly followed and adhered to in compliance with the guidelines issued by the Ministry of Gender, Labour and Social Development.
