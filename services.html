External Recruitment Department

Amazing Grace Labour Services Limited (AGLSL) strictly follows and complies with the Ministry of Gender, Labour and Social Development rules / guide lines to ensure that the recruitment process meets the required standards.

Amazing Grace Labour Services Limited

Amazing Grace Labour Services Limited (AGLSL) is in the business of labour sourcing and recruitment, targeting the sole purpose of placing labour in suitable organizations not only in Uganda, but also regionally and internationally. Incorporated on the 25th of July 2016, the company is officially registered and licensed by the ministry of Gender, Labour and Social Development to recruit and export labour.

We work for your future

The company aims at ensuring that the right employees are recruited, trained and placed in suitable and highly recognized organizations and institutions offering job opportunities in Uganda, African region and internationally.
